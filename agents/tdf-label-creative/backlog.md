## Backlog

### IDEA-001: Shareable Release Preview Cards
- **Hypothesis:** If artists can generate beautiful, shareable preview cards for their upcoming releases, they will share on Instagram/WhatsApp, driving organic app discovery.
- **ICE Score:** Impact 9 / Confidence 7 / Ease 6 = **378**
- **Experiment:** Build a "Share Preview" button on release detail screen. Generate a 1080x1920 image with album art, release date, and app QR code. Track: taps, shares, app opens from QR.
- **Metrics:** Share rate >5%, QR scans >2% of shares, D1 retention of users who scanned QR >30%
- **Effort:** 3 days
- **Status:** backlog

### IDEA-002: Streak Counter for Daily Uploads
- **Hypothesis:** If we show artists their consecutive daily upload streak with a visual reward, they'll form a daily habit and increase session frequency.
- **ICE Score:** Impact 7 / Confidence 8 / Ease 8 = **448**
- **Experiment:** Add a streak badge to home screen. Show flame icon with day count. Celebrate milestones (7, 30, 100 days) with confetti animation. Track: daily active users, streak length distribution.
- **Metrics:** DAU increase >15%, average streak length >3 days, D7 retention >25%
- **Effort:** 2 days
- **Status:** backlog

### IDEA-003: Collaboration Invite with Preview
- **Hypothesis:** If invitees can preview a collaboration track before accepting the invite, conversion rate will increase because they can assess the opportunity.
- **ICE Score:** Impact 8 / Confidence 6 / Ease 5 = **240**
- **Experiment:** When sending collaboration invite, include 30-second audio preview in the invite link. Recipient sees track name, preview button, and "Join Project" CTA before signing up. Track: invite open rate, preview play rate, signup conversion.
- **Metrics:** Invite conversion >15% (vs current baseline), preview play rate >60%
- **Effort:** 5 days
- **Status:** backlog

### IDEA-004: Release Countdown Widget
- **Hypothesis:** If artists can add a release countdown widget to their home screen, they'll open the app more frequently to check status and share the countdown.
- **ICE Score:** Impact 7 / Confidence 5 / Ease 4 = **140**
- **Experiment:** Build iOS home screen widget showing next release date and days remaining. Tapping opens app to release detail. Track: widget adds, tap-through rate, DAU.
- **Metrics:** Widget adoption >10% of active users, tap-through rate >20%, DAU increase >10%
- **Effort:** 4 days
- **Status:** backlog

### IDEA-005: "Who's Listening" Real-Time Feed
- **Hypothesis:** If artists see real-time listener activity (who played their track, from where), they'll feel more connected to their audience and check the app more often.
- **ICE Score:** Impact 8 / Confidence 6 / Ease 5 = **240**
- **Experiment:** Add a "Live" tab showing recent plays with location and time. Update every 60 seconds when app is open. Track: tab opens, session length, DAU.
- **Metrics:** Tab opened in >30% of sessions, session length increase >20%, DAU increase >10%
- **Effort:** 4 days
- **Status:** backlog

### IDEA-006: Referral Rewards for Artists
- **Hypothesis:** If artists earn credits or premium features for referring other artists who sign up and release music, they'll actively recruit from their network.
- **ICE Score:** Impact 9 / Confidence 5 / Ease 4 = **180**
- **Experiment:** Add "Invite & Earn" to settings. Referral code + unique link. Referrer gets $10 credit when referee releases first track. Track: invite sends, signup conversion, first-release conversion, cost per acquisition.
- **Metrics:** Invite rate >5% of users, referral conversion >10%, CPA < $20
- **Effort:** 5 days
- **Status:** backlog

### IDEA-007: Quick-Start Templates for New Users
- **Hypothesis:** If new users see genre-specific templates ("Reggaeton Release", "Salsa EP") instead of a blank slate, they'll complete their first upload faster and be more likely to return.
- **ICE Score:** Impact 7 / Confidence 8 / Ease 7 = **392**
- **Experiment:** On first open after onboarding, show template picker with 6 genre templates. Each pre-fills metadata fields. Track: template selection rate, first upload completion time, D1/D7 retention.
- **Metrics:** Template selection >70%, first upload within 24h >40%, D1 retention >45%
- **Effort:** 3 days
- **Status:** backlog

### IDEA-008: Weekly "Trending in Your City" Push
- **Hypothesis:** If we send a weekly push notification with trending releases from the user's city, they'll open the app to discover local artists and feel community connection.
- **ICE Score:** Impact 6 / Confidence 7 / Ease 6 = **252**
- **Experiment:** Weekly push: "3 artists are trending in Guayaquil this week 🔥". Opens to city-specific trending list. Track: push open rate, list engagement, DAU on push day.
- **Metrics:** Push open rate >15%, list scroll depth >50%, DAU spike >20% on push day
- **Effort:** 3 days
- **Status:** backlog
