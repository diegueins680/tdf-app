TDF HQ uses instagram_business_manage_messages in the Instagram Login review flow to open inbound Instagram conversations and send replies from the app UI.

Reviewer flow:
1. Open /social/instagram?review=1 and complete the full Instagram Login consent flow.
2. Keep the selected business/professional Instagram messaging asset visible on the setup screen.
3. Continue to /social/inbox?review=1.
4. Open a real inbound Instagram conversation.
5. Send a text reply from the TDF HQ app UI.
6. Show the exact same delivered message in the native Instagram client.
7. Delete or unsend that same message in the native Instagram client.
8. Return to /social/inbox?review=1 and keep the review checklist visible until the inbox auto-refresh removes the deleted/unsent message without a manual reload.

This permission is necessary because TDF HQ is the operational inbox used by the label team to reply to inbound Instagram messages and keep the in-app thread state synchronized with the native Instagram conversation lifecycle.
