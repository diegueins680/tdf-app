This video demonstrates the Instagram messaging flow used for Meta App Review.
First, we connect the app with Meta Login and grant the requested permissions for the selected professional/business Instagram account.
Next, we return to TDF HQ, open the review inbox, and compose a reply from the app UI.
Then we send the message, confirm delivery in the native Instagram client, delete or unsend the same message there, and wait for the inbox refresh to remove it without a manual reload.
