Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2025-12-27

If you want TDF Records to delete your Instagram or WhatsApp message data, follow the steps below. We will confirm the request and complete deletion within 30 days.

Request by email

  • Email info@tdfrecords.net.
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request
  • Include your Instagram handle and/or WhatsApp phone number.
  • Tell us whether you want full deletion or just message history removal.

Disconnect the app (optional)

You can revoke access anytime through Meta settings. This stops new data collection but does not delete previously stored data unless you also email us.

What we delete

  • Message content and reply history.
  • Sender identifiers and metadata associated with the conversation.
  • Ad or campaign referral metadata linked to those messages.

Contact

Questions? Email info@tdfrecords.net.
