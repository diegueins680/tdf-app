TDF Records Mobile App Privacy Policy

This policy explains how TDF Records handles information processed through the TDF Records mobile app, an Expo-based companion for schedules, bookings, artists, venues, QR contact exchange, and inventory workflows.

Key points

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Location, camera, and photo access are optional feature permissions.
  • You can request deletion or review of your data by email.

1. Information we collect

  • Account and profile identifiers needed to connect the app to your TDF Records access.
  • Content you enter in the app, such as names, emails, phone numbers, venue details, and artist data.
  • Inventory photos and related upload metadata when you choose to capture or attach images.
  • QR and vCard exchange data when you generate or scan contact-sharing codes.
  • Device location only when you grant permission to use venue discovery features.
  • Operational and security logs required to run the service, investigate failures, and protect the platform.

2. How we use information

  • To authenticate you, keep your session active, and load the TDF Records features you are allowed to use.
  • To sync schedules, parties, artists, venue data, social exchanges, and inventory workflows with TDF systems.
  • To power optional tools such as nearby venue search, QR scanning, and inventory photo capture.
  • To maintain platform security, prevent misuse, and troubleshoot product issues.
  • To meet legal, tax, accounting, or record-keeping duties when they apply.

3. Permissions used in the app

  • Camera: used for QR scanning and for taking inventory photos inside the app.
  • Photos or media library: used when you select an existing inventory image from your device.
  • Location: used to show nearby venues when you open venue explorer and approve access.
Optional access. You can deny camera, photos, or location permissions and still use the rest of the app, although the related feature will not work.

4. Storage, sharing, and retention

  • Authentication tokens and basic identity settings are stored locally on your device to keep the app usable between sessions.
  • Feature data is sent to TDF Records systems and service infrastructure needed to run the platform.
  • We share data only with processors and infrastructure required to deliver the service. We do not sell personal data.
  • We keep data only as long as needed for operations, support, legal compliance, and legitimate business records.

5. Your choices

  • Deny optional device permissions.
  • Clear your stored token or remove the app from your device.
  • Contact us for access, correction, privacy, or deletion requests.
