What to include in a support request

  • The platform you are using: iPhone or Android.
  • The email, phone number, or Party ID tied to your TDF account, if known.
  • A short description of the issue and the screen or feature involved.
  • Any error text, screenshot, or steps that reproduce the problem.

Common issue categories

  • Account access: problems with tokens, profile linkage, or permissions.
  • Bookings and schedules: issues loading sessions, venues, or appointments.
  • Inventory and uploads: trouble capturing photos or sending item updates.
  • QR and contact exchange: camera access or scanning problems in vCard workflows.
  • Privacy requests: deletion, access, or data-handling questions.
