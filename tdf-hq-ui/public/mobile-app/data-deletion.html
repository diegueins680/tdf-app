Request deletion of TDF Records mobile app data.

If you want TDF Records to review, delete, or anonymize data tied to your mobile app use, email us and include enough information to locate the account or workflow.

1. What to send us

  • Your full name and the email address tied to TDF Records, if available.
  • Your phone number or Party ID, if your account uses one.
  • The platform you used: iPhone or Android.
  • A short description of whether you want account deletion, workflow cleanup, or specific content removed.

2. What we can review for deletion

  • Profile and contact information associated with your mobile access where TDF Records controls that data.
  • Inventory photos or uploads submitted through the app.
  • QR or vCard exchange records associated with your account or Party ID.
  • Optional location-derived venue search data tied to service requests, where retained.

3. What may be retained

Some records may need to be retained for tax, accounting, fraud prevention, security, dispute handling, or other legal obligations. If that applies, we will explain what cannot be deleted and why.

4. Response timeline

We aim to confirm receipt and process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to identity verification and any legal retention duties.
