TDF InstG Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-27

Who we are

TDF Records operates TDF InstG Bot, a messaging assistant that helps us reply to Instagram and WhatsApp inquiries about our services, courses, and events.

Information we collect

  • Message content you send to us on Instagram or WhatsApp.
  • Sender identifiers (for example, user ID or phone number) and profile name.
  • Message metadata such as timestamps and ad or campaign referral details.
  • Basic technical logs used for security and troubleshooting.

How we use your information

  • To reply to your questions and provide information you request.
  • To understand which ads or campaigns generated inquiries.
  • To improve the quality, accuracy, and safety of our responses.
  • To comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

AI processing

We use OpenAI to draft responses. Message content and context may be sent to OpenAI solely to generate replies. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to operate the bot (such as Meta platforms, hosting, and OpenAI). We do not sell personal data.

Retention

We keep message data only as long as needed to respond, improve the service, and meet legal requirements. You can request deletion at any time.

Your choices

  • You can stop messaging us at any time.
  • You can request access or deletion of your data.

Data deletion

See our data deletion instructions: /data-deletion.html.

Contact

Email us at info@tdfrecords.net with any questions or requests.
